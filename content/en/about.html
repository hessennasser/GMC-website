About Us

Beginning of the Journey

Our journey in mining began in the 1980s with the trading of calcium carbonates, saffron stone, and Sinai stone, as well as breaking marble alabaster and Asiuti marble. The commercial journey with these mining materials continued until the early years of the current century. It was then that we started preparing the necessary production lines to produce some mining materials. We inaugurated Global Mining Factory in 2013, equipped with the latest production lines and necessary facilities for packaging and shipping.

In 2017, to meet market requirements and international standards, we installed multiple production lines to obtain mining products in all required sizes, aiming to satisfy market demands in this field.

Our Vision
